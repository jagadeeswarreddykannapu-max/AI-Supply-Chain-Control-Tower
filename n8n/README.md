# n8n Workflows

This folder contains the workflow automation developed for the AI Supply Chain Control Tower.

## Workflows

### 1. Inventory Intelligence
Monitors inventory levels and identifies stock risks.

### 2. Supplier Risk Intelligence
Evaluates supplier performance and operational risk.

### 3. AI Procurement Advisor
Provides procurement recommendations based on business rules.

### 4. Warehouse & Inventory Intelligence
Tracks warehouse operations and inventory KPIs.

### 5. Demand Planning Intelligence
Supports demand forecasting and planning.

### 6. Logistics & Transportation Intelligence
Monitors shipment and transportation performance.

### 7. Executive Decision Center
Consolidates KPIs and generates executive insights.
