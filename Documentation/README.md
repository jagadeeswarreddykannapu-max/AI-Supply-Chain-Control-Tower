# Project Overview

## Introduction

The AI Supply Chain Control Tower is a workflow automation project designed to improve visibility across key supply chain operations. The solution combines workflow automation, business intelligence, and executive decision support to monitor operational performance and generate actionable insights.

## Objectives

- Automate repetitive supply chain monitoring tasks.
- Improve visibility into procurement, inventory, warehouse, logistics, and supplier performance.
- Generate executive KPIs for faster decision-making.
- Reduce manual reporting through workflow automation.

- # Business Problem and Solution

## Business Problem

Supply chain teams often rely on multiple spreadsheets and manual reporting processes to monitor operational performance. This can delay decision-making, increase operational risk, and reduce visibility across different supply chain functions.

## Proposed Solution

The AI Supply Chain Control Tower centralizes operational data and automates key business processes using n8n workflows. It processes supply chain data, calculates business KPIs, and supports executive decision-making through automated insights and notifications.

## Business Value

- Reduced manual reporting effort.
- Faster identification of operational risks.
- Improved visibility across supply chain activities.
- Automated KPI monitoring.
- Enhanced executive decision support.
- # Workflow Explanation

The project consists of seven automation workflows.

## Inventory Intelligence

Monitors inventory levels and identifies stock risks.

## Supplier Risk Intelligence

Evaluates supplier performance and operational risk.

## AI Procurement Advisor

Supports procurement decisions using predefined business rules.

## Warehouse Intelligence

Tracks warehouse operations and inventory movement.

## Demand Planning Intelligence

Provides demand planning and forecasting support.

## Logistics & Transportation Intelligence

Monitors shipment status and transportation performance.

## Executive Decision Center

Consolidates outputs from all workflows into executive-level KPIs and recommendations.
