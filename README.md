# AI Supply Chain Control Tower

An AI-powered Supply Chain Control Tower built using n8n, JavaScript, Google Sheets, and Power BI to automate supply chain intelligence, risk monitoring, and executive decision support.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Modern supply chains generate large volumes of operational data across procurement, inventory, warehouse operations, logistics, and supplier management. Monitoring these processes manually can be time-consuming and delay decision-making.

This project demonstrates how workflow automation and business intelligence can be combined to build an AI-powered Supply Chain Control Tower. The solution automates supply chain monitoring, KPI calculation, risk analysis, and executive decision support.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- Inventory Intelligence
- Supplier Risk Intelligence
- AI Procurement Advisor
- Warehouse Intelligence
- Demand Planning Intelligence
- Logistics & Transportation Intelligence
- Executive Decision Center
